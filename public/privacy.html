Privacy Policy

g20tracker · Effective date: June 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how G20 (g20tracker), available at g20tracker.z-rex.net, handles information when you use the application.

Summary

G20 is a client-side web application. Your tester data is stored locally in your browser. We do not require an account and we do not operate a database that stores your testing records.

Information you provide

When using G20, you may enter information such as:

  • Tester names, platforms, and contact notes
  • Tags, descriptions, and daily check-in status
  • App settings (language, target tester count, default days)

This information is saved in your browser's localStorage and remains on your device unless you clear site data or export/delete it within the app.

Information we collect

G20 does not collect, transmit, or sell your tester records. App data stays in your browser unless you export it yourself.

We use Google Analytics (measurement ID: G-CC6VQM5S39) to understand how visitors use the website — for example page views, general location, browser type, and referral source. Google may set cookies and process this data according to the Google Privacy Policy. You can opt out via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Your hosting provider or CDN may also process standard web server logs (such as IP address, browser type, and request time) when you load the site.

Cookies

G20 does not set authentication cookies. Browser localStorage is used for app data persistence. Google Analytics may use cookies or similar technologies for site measurement as described above.

Data retention & deletion

  • Delete individual testers inside the app at any time
  • Clear all G20 data by removing site data for this domain in your browser settings
  • Exported CSV files are saved wherever you choose on your device

Children's privacy

G20 is a developer productivity tool and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will be revised when changes are made.

Contact

For privacy questions regarding g20tracker, please contact the site operator through the domain g20tracker.z-rex.net.
