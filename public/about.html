About G20

g20tracker · Last updated: June 2026

G20 (g20tracker) is a lightweight web app that helps Android developers manage Google Play mutual testing partnerships. When you exchange closed testing slots with other developers, G20 keeps your daily progress organized so you do not miss a day and break your streak.

What it does

  • Track each testing partner with name, platform, notes, and tags
  • Mark when a partner tested your app today (streak / active days)
  • Log when you tested their app (reply tracking, no fixed day limit)
  • Visual day-by-day progress grid with export to CSV
  • Multi-language UI and responsive layout for phone and desktop

How data is stored

G20 runs entirely in your browser. Tester records are saved to localStorage on your device. We do not operate a backend server and do not receive your testing data unless you choose to export it yourself.

Who it is for

Indie developers, small teams, and anyone participating in Google Play closed testing exchanges who needs a simple daily checklist — without accounts, subscriptions, or installation.

Contact & domain
